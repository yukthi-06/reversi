# Walkthrough: Increasing Coin Bevel

This document summarizes the visual adjustment made to the game coins to give them a more pronounced edge.

## Actions Taken
1. **Identified Bevel Logic:** Located the drawing layers inside `BoardView.drawCoin`. The bevel is created by the difference in size between the full-size "rim" circle and the slightly smaller "inner face" circle.
2. **Adjusted Radii:** Reduced the radius of the inner face layer from `85%` down to `70%` (`radius * 0.7f`). 
3. **Result:** Because the inner circle is now smaller, more of the underlying colored rim circle is exposed. This gives the coins a significantly thicker, more pronounced bevel edge, making the discs look chunkier and more defined on the board.
